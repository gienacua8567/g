You might catch some health benefits from it too.

Basil  - contains antioxidants that protects our cells from free radicals to prevent disease. It also contains  a good dose of Vitamin K that is involve in the formation of healthy tissues and bones.
Bay Leaf - is a good and healthier substitute to salt. Also, a rich source of vitamin A, vitamin C, iron, potassium, calcium, and magnesium.
Oregano - rich in antioxidants and have potent compounds that may help in reducing viral infection and decrease inflammation.
Thyme - helps improve our immune system as it is packed with Vitamin C and A as well as fiber and some minerals.
Cilantro - rich in antioxidants that may help lower your blood sugar, fight infections, and promote heart, brain, skin, and digestive health.
Sage - aside from being loaded with antioxidants it also contains properties that may support oral health. 
Rosemary - containd antioxidants and anti-inflamatory compunds. Also has fiber which aid in digestion as well nutrients and substances that help enhance memory and concentration.
Lemon Grass - rich in fiber that aids in good digestion; boosts metabollism and burns fat. It was also found to have properties that help in regulating high blood pressure.
Parsley - contains nutrients that protect your eyes and some cancer-fighting substances.
